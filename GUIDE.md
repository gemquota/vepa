# VEPA Engineering Guide: The Complexity Compendium (Final Master Version)

## 1. Core Parameter Synergies

Emergence in VEPA is rarely the result of a single slider. It is the **interference pattern** between multiple rules.

*   **Kinetic Cohesion (Force + Viscosity):** High Force (Attraction) usually leads to chaotic collisions. Adding **Viscosity** (0.8+) dampens these orbits, allowing particles to form stable "Molecules" that move as a single unit rather than a cloud.
*   **Ionic Locking (Charge Bias + Stiffness):** When **Charge Bias** is high, particles of opposite charges attract. By increasing **Stiffness**, you create a "Hard Shell" around these bonds, resulting in rigid, non-deformable crystal lattices.
*   **Social Synchronicity (Signal Resp + Pulse Rate):** When **Signal Resp** is enabled, particles respond to the "Blink" of their neighbors. Matching this with a high **Pulse Rate** creates rhythmic, flashing colonies that pulse with a shared "heartbeat."
*   **Genetic Drift (Mutation + Hybridization):** High **Mutation** in the DNA tab coupled with **Hybridization** in the Cell tab allows species to not only mix but to actively evolve new traits every time they "Breed" (Birth).

## 3. The Universal Theory of Emergence
In VEPA v0.8.5+, we have reached **Closed-Loop Simulation**. Matter now possesses its own internal state (CELL) independent of its spatial rules (DNA). 

### The Singularity Cycle
Black Holes are no longer just "deletion zones." They are massive gravity wells that clean the universe of "Inert Slop" (energy-less matter). By adjusting the **Hawking Radiation** parameters in the Singularity tab, you can turn Black Holes into "Particle Forges" that emit new, high-energy particles once they reach a critical mass. This creates a cycle of **Collapse and Rebirth**.

## 4. Hybridization & Genetic Ecosystems
The **Hybridization** parameter in the Cell tab allows different species to cross-breed.

### Symbiotic Engineering
*   **Species Red:** High Force, Low Metabolism (The Hunters).
*   **Species Blue:** High Stiffness, High Signal (The Builders).
*   **Hybridization Synergy:** If Red and Blue breed, they create **Species Purple**. The hybrid will have the architectural strength of the Builders but the aggressive movement of the Hunters. Over millions of cycles, your "Species Palette" will transform from pure colors into a diverse, emergent spectrum of life.

## 5. Resurrection Mechanics
Particles that run out of energy turn **Inert (Gray)**. They stop applying their DNA rules and drift aimlessly.

### Stellar Recharging
If an Inert cluster drifts near a **Star**, it will begin to absorb energy. Once it crosses the **15% threshold**, it "Wakes Up." You can engineer "Rescue Organisms" (High Signal, High Force) that actively push inert matter back toward stars to revive them.

## 6. Advanced Singularity Tactics
*   **Event Horizon Snaring:** Set the **Horizon Scale** to 2.0. This creates a "Sticky" black hole that catches orbiting matter long before they touch the core.
*   **Evaporative Chaos:** Set **Evaporation** to High. This makes black holes short-lived "Flash Events" that pull the universe into a point and then immediately vanish, scattering matter everywhere.
