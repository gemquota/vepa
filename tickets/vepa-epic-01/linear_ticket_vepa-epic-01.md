# Description

## Problem to solve
The VEPA engine is currently at v1.1.0 with basic physics and render capabilities. To achieve true emergent artificial life and complex physical simulations, we need to implement Sensing, Communication, Advanced Motion/Matter, Biology/Chemistry, and Global Environmental Laws.

## Solution
Execute the roadmap defined in the PICKLE_JAR.md across 4 major batches (3-6), transforming the engine into a multi-layered simulation of complex systems.

## Implementation Details
- Batch 3: Sensing & Communication (v1.2)
- Batch 4: Motion, Matter & EM Depth (v1.3)
- Batch 5: Biology, Chemistry & Memory (v1.4)
- Batch 6: Global, Chaos, Laws & Final Polish (v1.5+)
